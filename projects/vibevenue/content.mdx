VibeVenue is our company website that showcases our innovative web-based kiosk software solutions for events and venues. We specialize in creating engaging, interactive experiences that enhance guest experiences at various occasions.

## Products and Services

Our kiosk software includes:

- **Interactive Games**: Small, fun games designed for event attendees to enjoy during downtime.
- **AI Photobooths**: Advanced photobooth experiences powered by AI for creative and memorable photo sessions.
- **Custom Kiosk Solutions**: Tailored web-based kiosks for check-ins, information displays, and more.


## Admin Panel Features

The website includes a comprehensive admin panel that allows administrators to:

- **Message Management**: View messages sent through the contact page and reply directly using AWS SES emails.
- **Rate Limiting**: Implements rate limits to prevent spam and ensure fair usage.
- **Webhook Notifications**: Runs periodic webhooks to notify admins about unread messages.
- **Blog Post Authoring**: Add and edit blog posts with OpenAI integration to assist in content creation.
- **Dynamic OpenGraph Images**: Automatically generates custom OpenGraph images for blog posts to enhance social media sharing.

VibeVenue empowers event organizers and venue managers to create unforgettable experiences through technology, combining ease of use with powerful backend capabilities.
